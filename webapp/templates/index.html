Key Features

Crime Detection

Uses a ResNet-based deep learning model to classify videos/images into different crime categories.

Suspect Identification

Extracts and saves faces of potential suspects from crime footage for later identification.

Weapon Detection

Identifies potential weapons in crime scenes to assess threat levels and inform authorities.

SMS Alerts

Sends immediate notifications when crimes are detected to authorities or designated contacts.

History Tracking

Maintains a comprehensive history of all detections for review and analysis.

Live Camera Integration

Supports real-time crime detection through webcam or connected security cameras.
